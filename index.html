About us

Step into our oasis nestled in the heart of Seattle, where the crisp aroma of freshly brewed coffee intertwines harmoniously with the lively essence of lush greenery. At Greens Coffee Shop, we pride ourselves on offering more than just a cup of joe — we provide an experience that rejuvenates both body and soul.

As you step through our doors, you'll be enveloped in the tranquility of nature, creating the perfect backdrop for a moment of relaxation or a burst of creativity. Each sip of our meticulously crafted brews serves as a catalyst for productivity, awakening your senses and fueling your passion.

Our verdant sanctuary is more than just a coffee shop; it's a haven where the synergy of caffeine and foliage intertwine to elevate your workday to new heights. Whether you're seeking a quiet retreat to unwind or a vibrant space to spark inspiration, Greens Coffee Shop welcomes you to indulge in a sensory journey unlike any other.
